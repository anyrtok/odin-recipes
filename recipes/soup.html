Pumpkin Soup

Description

Ingredients

  • 1.59 kg butternut squash
  • 1 tbsp avocado oil
  • 1 small yellow onion, halved
  • 0.25 tsp ground ginger

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 425 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Transfer the butternut squash to a baking tray cut side up, coat with oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  3. Pour the soup into bowls and garnish with pepitas, parsley and cracked black pepper.
