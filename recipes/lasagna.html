Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is the perfect meal, and of all of the recipes I’ve tried over the years, this is the perfect lasagna.

It’s flavorful and satisfying, but also easy to make. It’s delicious as-is, but you can make it your own by swapping in your favorite ingredients

Ingredients

  • 500 g ground beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • 680 g Marinara Sauce
  • 9 lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Place a deep pan or dutch oven over medium/high heat
  2. Add 1/4 cup wine and stir for 2 minutes
  3. Make Cheese Sauce
  4. Make Lasagna
